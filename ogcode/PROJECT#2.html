WHO ARE WE


Unapologetic, unabashed and unafraid, Villain is made for the men who are true to themselves and their style, however twisted it may be. Even a good man can turn bad when crisis comes knocking and for that man who’s bold, bad & ready for it all… we have Villain. A celebration of the antagonist that lies amongst all of us, Villain is here to offer in-your-face, bold and stylish products that invoke an intriguing and sexy demeanor and break the trend. We offer a range of fragrances, apparels and accessories, each masculine and unique in its own way. From perfumes, sunglasses, gym-wear, hoodies, men's jewelry, boxers and much more, the brand has introduced clutter-breaking products with unique USPs that would appeal to the youth of India and its ever-evolving needs. Ex-bad boys of Beardo, VILLAIN is the latest venture of entrepreneurs Ashutosh Valani & Priyank Shah, in partnership with KGF-famed Rocking Star Yash and is now owned by MENSA Brands.
